Encore Creative Arts Camp Experience!

FALL Session November 20-22

Encore Creative Arts Camps will host Visual and Creative Arts Camps this Fall, Monday-Wednesday during Thanksgiving Break.

Students will sample some of the student's favorite camps at Encore. Drawing and Anime, Miniature Craft, Painting, Theatre, Photography & Film, and a few Science Experiments!

Early drop off at 8:30 am and After care til 5:15 available.

WINTER December 18-22

Come and get creative this winter break with Encore Creative Arts Camps Samplers! Students will enjoy all of the favorite camps in a daily schedule with different classes each class period. Our favorite Encore Camps including (but not limited to): Anime, Photography, Film, Theatre, Miniature Craft, Science, and much more...

Students will enjoy learning new skills, honing their crafts, socializing and making new friends. There will be outdoor time, weather permitting.

Early drop off begins at 8:30, and After Care is available
